## The Architecture Shift: Creating the API Web Platform

Moving a machine learning engine out of a local command-line script and onto the web marks a massive milestone in a developer's lifecycle. To make our mental health predictive engine accessible to external systems or mental health professionals, we shifted our architecture into a full Application Programming Interface (API) platform. This architectural layer transforms our model from a static local asset into a live service capable of securely accepting structural inputs over network layers and serving instant classifications.

---

## The Technical Core: Wrapping the Inference Engine
